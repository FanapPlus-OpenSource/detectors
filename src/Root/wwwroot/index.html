Kafka - Brokers

/api/kafka/clusters

List of known Kafka clusters

/api/kafka/cluster/CLUSTER_ID/metadata

Full metadata for a cluster

/api/kafka/cluster/CLUSTER_ID/brokers

List of brokers in a cluster

Kafka - Topics

/api/kafka/cluster/CLUSTER_ID/topics

List of topics and the number of partitions

/api/kafka/cluster/CLUSTER_ID/topic-partitions

Detailed list of all partitions for all topics, with leader and ISRs

/api/kafka/cluster/CLUSTER_ID/topic/TOPIC_ID/partitions

Detailed list of partitions for a single topic, with leader and ISRs

/api/kafka/cluster/CLUSTER_ID/topic/TOPIC_ID/offsets/total

Sum of high watermark offsets

/api/kafka/cluster/CLUSTER_ID/topic/TOPIC_ID/offsets/total/rate/[DURATION]

Per-minute change rate on sum of high watermark offsets. Duration can be like '15s', '5m', '1h'

Kafka - Consumer Groups

/api/kafka/cluster/CLUSTER_ID/groups

/api/kafka/cluster/CLUSTER_ID/topic/TOPIC_ID/consumer/CONSUMER_GROUP_ID/commit/total

/api/kafka/cluster/CLUSTER_ID/topic/TOPIC_ID/consumer/CONSUMER_GROUP_ID/commit/total/rate/[DURATION]
